Infrastructure

Naming System

We currently have backups of other projects priv.gg, Lagout, and 2thi.cc these are kept in their original state. Any loose files are in camel case and underscores instead of spaces example: encyclopediaAnimalBehaviorSecondEditionOliverWalter this makes it easier to keep track of and organize our files every folder has dir infront of it to help with differentiation when parsing
